Studio Practice

Every session begins before the first take.

Good recording is equal parts preparation and instinct: understanding the room, choosing the right signal path, protecting the artist’s confidence, and knowing when not to interfere.

Biography

Engineer by craft. Musician by instinct. Technologist by curiosity.

Lewis Fawcett is a recording engineer, producer, multi-instrumentalist, and TouchDesigner artist whose work sits between studio precision and live musical energy. His practice is built around the idea that technology should support feeling — not flatten it.

Whether placing microphones inside a piano, shaping a live performance, producing dialogue, or building interactive visual systems, Lewis approaches each project as a complete environment: technical, musical, spatial, and human.
